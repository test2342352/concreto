The Dabaa central

Project information

  • Category: Roads and Bridges

The Dabaa central is a dual road and one of the main roads in the Arab Republic of Egypt, operated by the National Company for Road Construction and Development, connecting the Egyptian capital of Cairo with the city of Daba in Matrouh governorate.
The first stage of the Circular Road begins in the Bichtel region parallel to the 26 July axis, crossing the Mansuriyah Road, passing through the Sheikh Zayed and October areas to intersect with the Cairo/Alexandria desert road in Kilo 39. The second phase is 30 km from Kilo 39 to Dhaba, crossing the regional ring road and passing through the city of Alamina. The hub aims to connect several cities with various governorates, including the 6th of October City and Sheikh Zayed City in the eastern Cairo and central delta region, and reduce the traffic density on the 26 July hub by a 40% proportion, thereby facilitating movement within the central Giza area at engineers, Diqi and Agouza and unshackling traffic in Lebanon Square.

Eldhaba Desalination Station

Project information

  • Category: Water Stations

The desalination plant in the city of Dhaba in Kilo 120 east of Marsa Matrouh was implemented with a capacity of 40 thousand cubic metres per day at a total cost of 2 billion and 300 million pounds to serve 200 thousand villagers and Najas in the city of Dhaba.

The station was built on an area of 40 thousand square meters, which is 10 acres. The plant's project includes a marine harbour and three reservoirs with a capacity of 10 thousand cubic metres per reservoir, including a saltwater tank and a desalinated water tank as well as the R.o desalination unit, including electrical control rooms, the productive water pumps building, the generator building, transformers and the interconnection network between the project units, which include saltwater feeding and ultra-saline water drainage, as well as administrative buildings
